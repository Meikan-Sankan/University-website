Frequently Asked Questions

Is Daystar accredited by CUE? +
Yes, Daystar University is fully accredited by the Commission for University Education (CUE) and recognized internationally. All our programs meet global standards.
Does the university offer scholarships? +
We offer both merit-based and need-based scholarships to eligible students. Application is done through the admissions office.
What sports can I join? +
We have 13+ sports teams including Soccer, Basketball, Handball, Athletics, Tennis, Badminton, Chess, Swimming, and more!
Are there international partnerships? +
Yes! We have 50+ international partnerships with universities worldwide, offering student exchange programs and research opportunities.
